Page Transition Suggestions

Recommended Page Transitions:

  • ✨ Fade: Simple opacity transition (300ms)
  • ➡️ Slide: Content slides in from side (400ms)
  • 📱 Mobile Push: New page pushes old page out (500ms)
  • 🔄 Rotate: 3D rotation between pages (600ms)
  • 🎯 Zoom: Scale in/out transition (400ms)

Scroll-Triggered Animations

Popular scroll-triggered animation options:

  • 📜 Fade Up: Elements fade in and slide up as you scroll
  • ↔️ Slide In: Content slides in from left/right on scroll
  • 🔢 Counter: Numbers count up when scrolled into view
  • 📊 Progress Bars: Bars fill up when visible
  • ✨ Parallax: Background moves slower than foreground
